Choose Your CPU

The CPU is often called the brain of the computer. It handles instructions, calculations, and system operations.

Intel Core i5

Good for everyday use, school, and general multitasking.

  • Balanced performance
  • Good for students
  • Works well for basic programming

Intel Core i7

Better for demanding tasks and heavier multitasking.

  • Higher performance
  • Good for editing and development
  • Better for long term use

AMD Ryzen 5

Strong value and performance for everyday users and gamers.

  • Great multitasking
  • Popular for mid range builds
  • Reliable performance

AMD Ryzen 7

Excellent for advanced workloads and stronger overall performance.

  • More processing power
  • Good for content creation
  • Strong high end option

What makes a CPU important?

A stronger CPU can improve responsiveness, multitasking, and processing speed. Users who browse the web or write documents may not need a high end processor, while users who code, game, or edit video may benefit from one.
