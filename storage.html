Choose Your Storage

Storage keeps your operating system, applications, documents, and media saved even when the computer is turned off.

500 GB SSD

Fast startup times and enough space for everyday use.

  • Fast performance
  • Good for school or office work

1 TB SSD

A strong option for users who want speed and more room for files and software.

  • Fast load times
  • Better for games and larger apps

2 TB HDD

Provides more space but slower performance than an SSD.

  • Larger capacity
  • Better for mass storage

SSD vs HDD

SSDs are faster and more common in modern computers. HDDs are still useful when users need a lot of storage at a lower cost. Many systems combine both to balance speed and space.
