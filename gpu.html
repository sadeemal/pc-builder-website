Select Your GPU

The GPU processes graphics and visual content. It is especially important for gaming, 3D work, design, and video editing.

NVIDIA RTX 4060

A good choice for modern gaming and visual tasks at a mid range level.

NVIDIA RTX 4070

Stronger performance for higher graphics settings and demanding applications.

AMD Radeon RX 7600

A solid alternative for gaming and users looking for a different value option.

Do all users need a GPU?

Not always. Many users can rely on integrated graphics for browsing, streaming, and office tasks. A dedicated GPU becomes more important for gaming, design, rendering, and video production.
